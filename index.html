Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

For a workforce that is capable of and ready for the future,Bangladesh 2.0 encourages digital growth,entrepreneurship, and tech-driven solutions.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly development is ensured by a focus on climate resilience, carbon reduction,and green energy.

Infrastructure

Megaprojects increase connectivity, trade, and access to essential services in a climate-resilient manner.

Empowerment

Gender inequalities, youth employment, and rural development are all addressed by inclusive policies to promote equitable growth.
